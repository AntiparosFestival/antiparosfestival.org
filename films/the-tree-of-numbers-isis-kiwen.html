Directors Statement

I don't think I will ever forget the experience I lived when creating The Tree of Numbers. I cannot forget the children and people crying for our departure after shooting in the small town of Cusihuiriachi, Chihuahua, México. I had given life, by donating a kidney but I found an even grater happiness in sharing and including the people of Cusihuiriachi in this production. After this life changing experience, every film I make takes me on a journey of self discovery and understanding of humankind.
